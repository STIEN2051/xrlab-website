OUR PURPOSE

Why We Exist

Technology is no longer confined to screens.

The future belongs to immersive environments where humans interact naturally with digital spaces. At the AR/VR Research Lab, we prepare students to lead that transformation by providing an ecosystem for experimentation, collaboration, and research.

From virtual simulations and interactive experiences to spatial interfaces and intelligent digital environments, we encourage students to think beyond conventional software and engineer the next generation of immersive computing.
