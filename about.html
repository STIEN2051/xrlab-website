WHO WE ARE

The AR/VR Research Lab at Geethanjali College of Engineering & Technology is a multidisciplinary research and innovation center committed to advancing immersive technologies through education, experimentation, and collaborative research.

Center Overview

Established with the vision of preparing students for the next generation of computing, the lab brings together researchers, developers, designers, and innovators to explore how immersive technologies can solve real-world problems and redefine digital interaction.

The lab serves as an environment where theoretical concepts evolve into practical solutions through hands-on development, scientific inquiry, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Our Story

Immersive technologies are rapidly transforming industries across healthcare, education, manufacturing, architecture, entertainment, and communication.

Recognizing this shift, the AR/VR Research Lab was established to create an environment where students could explore these emerging technologies beyond the classroom. Today, the lab has grown into a collaborative space where research meets creativity and ideas become impactful experiences.

Our Philosophy

We believe innovation is driven by curiosity. Rather than focusing solely on learning software or programming languages, we encourage students to understand people, interactions, and experiences.

Technology should feel natural.

Seamless digital integration into human life and physical spaces.

Design should feel invisible.

Interfaces that get out of the way to let user intent flow freely.

Research should create impact.

Solving real-world problems and advancing societal capabilities.

🔭 Vision

To become a leading academic center for immersive technology research, innovation, and experiential learning while contributing meaningful solutions to society through Extended Reality.

🚀 Mission
  • ✓ Advance research in immersive technologies.
  • ✓ Encourage interdisciplinary innovation across departments.
  • ✓ Promote experiential and project-based learning.
  • ✓ Foster global academic engagement through conferences and publications.
  • ✓ Build solutions that positively impact society and industry.
Our Culture

We believe the best ideas emerge when diverse perspectives come together.

Our lab promotes openness, collaboration, experimentation, continuous learning, and responsible innovation. Every project is viewed as an opportunity to discover something new.
